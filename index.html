Articles

Select a date between July 2015 and yesterday (July 2015 was the year wikipedia started making its data public and yesterday because they will not release the current day's data until the day ends). Chose the number of articles you would like to be displayed. Select a language. You may save your settings by clicking "save". You may reset your settings by clicking "reset". To start the search click on "submit". Be wary as if you click reset you must enter a new valid date. If you decide not to save, the default settings will be used (Language: English, Number of Articles: 10, Date: yesterday)
